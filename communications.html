Communications Technology

Communications technology courses offer students hands-on experience in areas such as digital media, broadcasting, graphic design, and telecommunications. These courses provides learners with skills in audio-visual production, video editing, web design, and network systems. These courses ensures that students are well-prepared for careers in media, information technology, and communication industries.

Projects

Spot the Differences

During this Communications Technology unit in this course, I completed an assignment focused on photoshopping skills, where I created a Spot the Differences puzzle using Adobe Photoshop. This project required me to take original images and digitally alter them to include subtle changes, such as removing or shifting objects, adjusting colours, or resizing elements, to create a second version with hidden differences. Through this assignment, I developed technical skills such as using layers, selection tools, and image editing techniques in Photoshop, as well as soft skills like attention to detail, and creativity. I found the experience fun and engaging because it challenged me to think critically and pay close attention to visual elements.
