About Technological Studies

What is the Technological Studies department?

Technological Studies is a department at Bill Hogarth Secondary School (BHSS) that offers comprehensive and diverse courses related to technology. It offers a dynamic and hands-on exploration of how technology shapes our world. Throughout the semester, we engaged with a variety of sub-units that develop both practical and critical thinking skills.

Units offered

This semester, there were six key units: Communications Technology, Technology Design, Healthcare Technology, Construction Technology, Transportation Technology, and Computer Technology.
