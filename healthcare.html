Healthcare Technology

Healthcare technology courses introduce students to the basics of the healthcare field, including how technology is used to support patient care and health services. These courses help students explore careers in health and wellness while developing essential skills like communication, safety, and problem-solving. Students gain an understanding of how healthcare systems work and how technology plays a role in supporting medical professionals and improving patient outcomes through hands-on activities.

Projects

Human Body Systems Functions Presentations

During this unit, I completed a collaborative research and presentation assignment on the excretory system as part of a health care project. We researched how the excretory system functions to remove waste from the body and maintain internal balance. We organized our findings into five main topics: an overview of the system, common symptoms and diagnostic methods, environmental and societal impacts like dehydration and pollution, related healthcare careers such as nephrologists and dialysis technicians, and fun facts to keep the audience engaged. We presented our research using a Google Slides presentation that included title slides, visuals, and concise bullet points. This assignment helped strengthen my research, collaboration, and public speaking skills while giving me a deeper understanding of the human body systems.
